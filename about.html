About Us


Our Mission


Essentual's mission is to unlock your self-care practice with aromatherapy products & create a path to deeper self-awareness in the easiest way possible.


Our Vision


Essentual's vision is to be a household name for organic, natural products that promote physical health and well-being. It's important to us that we have a positive impact on our society by creating easy access to self-care for everyone.We focus on using nature’s elements to combine ancient holistic wellness practice with current research to build a healthier future.
