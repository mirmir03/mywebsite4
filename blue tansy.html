Though blue tansy is harvested from tiny yellow flowers in the chamomile family, the naturally occurring constituent chamazulene turns the powerfully pure Blue Tansy essential oil a beautifully rich blue color during the steam distillation process. Native to Morocco, blue tansy is a rare botanical that is most commonly known for its use in high-end, luxury beauty products. For years Young Living members have enjoyed the benefits of Blue Tansy essential oil in some of our most popular blends, including Valor, Peace & Calming, and JuvaFlex. Now Blue Tansy is available as a single oil for you to diffuse, apply topically, and share!
